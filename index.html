About Me

My Introduction!

My name is Duy, pronounced like “do we.” I was born in Vietnam on October 16, 2007, and later moved to the U.S. when I was four!

I developed an interest in LEGO when I was young and that evolved into building computers and, later, programming. I gained a strong interest in programming because I like that it encourages creativity, many of my hobbies reflect on that ideology. Being able to build whatever I want with technology piqued my interest in early high school.

Outside of coding, I also enjoy playing games with my friends, some notable mentions being Fortnite, Minecraft, and Roblox. I love fashion, having spent thousands of dollars on clothes over the years, and also can't live without music despite being deaf on my right! When I'm not socializing? AirPods are in. My latest hobby is learning, modifying and working on cars. A little fun fact is that my first car was a 2014 Scion FR-S, and it was a manual!
